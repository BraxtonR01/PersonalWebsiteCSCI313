About Me

My name is Braxton Rolle, and I am a current student studying Computer Science at NDSU. I am from Hebron, ND and I am currently in my 6th semester of college. I transferred after receiving my associates in Computer Science at BSC. I plan to attend graduate school here as well in pursuit of my Masters in Computer Science. I am incredibly passionate about video games and their development, as well as general software development. I am hoping to work as a video game or software developer in the future.
